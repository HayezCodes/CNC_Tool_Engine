## GROOVING ENGINE

Inputs:

* material_group
* operation_type (OD / ID / part-off)
* width (narrow / medium / wide)
* stability (good / average / poor)

Logic:

* part-off → HIGH toughness
* narrow → HIGH break risk → increase toughness
* poor stability → toughness + PVD
* wide → allow wear resistance

Outputs:

* insert style
* toughness level
* wear level
* coating
* chipbreaker direction
* supplier queries

---

## THREADING ENGINE

Inputs:

* material_group
* thread_type (internal / external)
* pitch (fine / medium / coarse)
* tolerance (standard / high)

Logic:

* fine pitch → wear
* coarse → toughness
* internal → slight toughness increase
* high tolerance → wear

Outputs:

* laydown vs full profile
* coating
* toughness/wear balance
* chip control
* supplier queries

---

## DRILL ENGINE

Inputs:

* material_group
* diameter (small / medium / large)
* depth (shallow / medium / deep)
* tolerance (standard / precision)

Logic:

* small → solid carbide
* large → indexable
* deep → coolant-through
* stainless/super alloys → toughness
* precision → wear

Outputs:

* drill type
* coating
* geometry style
* supplier queries
